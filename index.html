What do you want to know?

Do I need a certain skill level?

Absolutely not! We welcome everyone, no matter where you are on your path to health and wellness. We all want the same thing: to take our strength and energy to the next level, whatever that level may be.

How do I book a class?

We have an online and mobile app appointment scheduler. This allows you to sign up for group fitness classes, reserve a studio or court, or schedule an appointment with the zen center. It’s all confidential, safe, and easy.

Do I have to sign an annual contract?

You can choose an annual, bi-annual, or monthly plan. Price varies depending on the option you select. We also offer short-term weekly “tryouts” or “vacation memberships.”

Is there a family discount?

We are currently offering family discounts if everyone in the family signs up within two weeks. This does not apply to friends.
